Arson
Alias Arson Iceaxe
Culture Free Folk
Book(s) A Storm of Swords (mentioned)

Arson, better known as Arson Iceaxe, was a wildling raider.

History

He tried to cross the Wall by digging through it's base. When he was midway under the ice, he was discovered by a patrol from the Nightfort. Instead of trying to stop the wildling, they decided to wall him in alive. The legend says that by listening the ear against the Wall, one can still hear the sound of Arson's axe digging away at the ice. Since this time, the Night's Watch sends infrequent patrols along the base of the Wall on it's northern face to look for traces of excavation.[1]
