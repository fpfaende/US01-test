Big Belly Ben
Alias Big Belly Ben
Allegiance Kingswood Brotherhood
Book(s) A Storm of Swords (mentioned)

Big Belly Ben was a famous member of the Kingswood Brotherhood. During battle against the forces of King Aerys II Targaryen, Ben was about to smash in the head of Lord Sumner Crakehall. Jaime Lannister rescued Lord Sumner, but Ben was able to escape.[1]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Tom Sevenstrings sings a silly song about Big Belly Ben and the High Septon's goose.[2]
