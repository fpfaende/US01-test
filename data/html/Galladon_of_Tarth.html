Galladon Tarth
Allegiance House Tarth
Culture Stormlands
Born In either 275 AC, 276 AC or 277 AC[1]
Died In either 284 AC or 285 AC[1]
Book(s) A Feast for Crows (mentioned)

Galladon Tarth was the only son of Lord Selwyn Tarth of Evenfall. He drowned when he was eight years old.[2]
