Gorghan of Old Ghis
Culture Ghiscari
Book(s) A Feast for Crows (mentioned)

Gorghan of Old Ghis was an ancient scholar from Ghis.

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

After Samwell Tarly tells Archmaester Marwyn that Maester Aemon believed Daenerys Targaryen to be the fulfillment of a prophecy, Marwyn quotes Gorghan on the nature of prophecy.[1]

Quotes about Gorghan

Gorghan of Old Ghis once wrote that a prophecy is like a treacherous woman. She takes your member in her mouth, and you moan with the pleasure of it and think, how sweet, how fine, how good this is... and then her teeth snap shut and your moans turn to screams. That is the nature of prophecy, said Gorghan. Prophecy will bite your prick off every time.[1]

- Archmaester Marwyn quoting Gorghan's views on prophecy
