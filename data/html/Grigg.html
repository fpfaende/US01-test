Grigg the Goat is a free folk raider.

Appearance and Character

Grigg has a long blond braid.[1] He is gregarious.[2]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Grigg is a part of Jarl's party that scales the Wall as part of the rear assault on Castle Black. He leads a team of four climbers to the top of the Wall. He tells Jon Snow that he hopes to visit the Isle of Faces someday.[2]
