Lady Janna Fossoway is the wife of Ser Jon Fossoway.[1] She was born into House Tyrell and is the sister of Mace Tyrell, Lord of Highgarden.[1]

Appearance and Character

Janna is buxom[2] and enjoys gossip.[3]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Janna is among Margaery Tyrell's retinue when she comes to King's Landing.[2] She shares gossip with Sansa Stark.[3] Ser Kevan Lannister dances with Janna during Sansa's wedding to Tyrion Lannister.[4]

A Feast for Crows

Lady Janna remains in King's Landing after the departure of most of the lords and ladies from the Reach. She hawks with Queen Margaery.[5] When Queen Regent Cersei Lannister's party travels to the Great Sept of Baelor, Janna and Meredyth Crane wear flowers in their hair and are escorted by the Redwyne twins.[6]
