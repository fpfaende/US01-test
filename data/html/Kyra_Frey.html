Kyra Frey
Allegiance House Frey
Born In or between 262 AC and 277 AC[1]
Spouse Ser Garse Goodbrook
Book(s) A Clash of Kings (appendix)
A Storm of Swords (mentioned)
A Feast for Crows (appendix)

Kyra Frey is a member of House Frey, the daughter of Ser Jared Frey and Alys Frey. Kyra is married to Ser Garse Goodbrook, with whom she has two children; Walder and Jeyne Goodbrook.[2]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Kyra's husband, Ser Garse Goodbrook is killed at the Red Wedding, making Kyra a widow.[3][4]
