Lum
Allegiance House Lannister
Culture westermen
Book(s) A Storm of Swords (appears)
A Feast for Crows (mentioned)

Lum is a guard in service to House Lannister.

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Lum is one of the guards who are guarding the apartments of Lord Tywin Lannister, the Hand of the King, in the Tower of the Hand the night Tyrion Lannister sneaks inside and kills Tywin.[1]

A Feast for Crows

When feeling a call of nature, Lum finds Lord Tywin Lannister dead on the privy.[2]
