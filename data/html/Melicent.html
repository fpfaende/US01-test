Helicent
Title Septa
Book(s) A Feast for Crows (mentioned)
A Dance with Dragons (appendix)

Helicent is a septa of the Faith of the Seven in King's Landing. She is one of the Most Devout at the Great Sept of Baelor.[1]

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

Septas Moelle, Aglantine, Helicent, and Nysterica examine Margaery Tyrell and discover that her maidenhead is not intact, giving grounds for Margaery's arrest by the Faith on charges of infidelity.[2]

Behind the Scenes

In Cersei X of A Feast for Crows, Helicent is erroneously called "Melicent".

Although Septas Helicent, Moelle, Aglantine and Unella are listed as members of the Most Devout in the Appendix of A Feast for Crows, the Appendix of A Dance with Dragons does not, possibly indicating that the former appendix is incorrect and none of the septas belong to the Most Devout.
