Roland Crakehall is the Lord of Crakehall and the head of House Crakehall.[1]

History

During the Sack of King's Landing, Roland, Ser Elys Westerling, and other westermen entered the Red Keep's throne room shortly after Ser Jaime Lannister of the Kingsguard killed King Aerys II Targaryen. Roland informed Jaime that King's Landing had fallen to the forces of his father, Lord Tywin Lannister. Jaime seated himself on the Iron Throne and waited for more men to arrive, which turned out to be Lord Eddard Stark with his northmen.[2]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

Roland is rumored to have died at the Battle of Oxcross,[3] but Lord Crakehall is present at King Joffrey I Baratheon's first audience after the Battle of the Blackwater.[4]

A Feast for Crows

Ser Jaime Lannister thinks Roland would make an able Hand of the King.[5]

Quotes

Roland: The castle is ours, ser, and the city.

Jaime: Tell them the Mad King is dead. Spare all those who yield and hold them captive.
Roland: Shall I proclaim a new king as well?

Jaime: Proclaim who you bloody well like.[2]

- Roland and Jaime Lannister
