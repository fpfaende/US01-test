Sweetport Sound

From A Wiki of Ice and Fire
Jump to: navigation, search

Sweetport Sound is the seat of House Sunglass in the crownlands.[1] Sweetport Sound is sworn to Dragonstone,[2] although its location along Blackwater Bay or the narrow sea has not been revealed. Ser Triston of Tally Hill is sworn to Lord Guncer Sunglass,[3] so Tally Hill may be located near Sweetport Sound.

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

After the burning of Lord Guncer Sunglass, his brother flees Stannis Baratheon and sails for Volantis.[4]
