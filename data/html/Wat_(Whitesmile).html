Whitesmile Wat is a singer with a high, sweet voice.[1]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

During his stay at Harrenhal as part of Lord Tywin Lannister's army, Wat possibly impregnates the jailer's wife.[2]

A Feast for Crows

Genna Lannister brings Wat with her from Lannisport to Riverrun for the siege.[3] He returns to the west after the fall of Riverrun, joining Ser Forley Prester's force.[1]
