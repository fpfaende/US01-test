Zharaq zo Loraq
Alias The Liberator
Allegiance House of Loraq
Culture Ghiscari
Book(s) A Dance with Dragons (mentioned)

Zharaq zo Loraq is a famous Ghiscari citizen of the city of Meereen. He is an ancestor of Hizdahr zo Loraq.[1]
